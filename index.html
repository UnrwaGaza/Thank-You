The Ongoing Crisis in Gaza

The people of Gaza, especially the children, are currently facing an unprecedented humanitarian crisis due to the ongoing conflict. Families are struggling to access basic necessities such as food, clean water, and medical supplies. The situation has left countless children traumatized, injured, and in dire need of assistance. Your support can make a significant difference in providing essential aid and bringing hope to these vulnerable young lives.

About Us

UNRWA (United Nations Relief and Works Agency for Palestine Refugees in the Near East) is a UN agency that supports the relief and human development of Palestinian refugees. Our mission is to help Palestine refugees achieve their full potential in human development under the difficult circumstances in which they live.
